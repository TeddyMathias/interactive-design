It's now the year 2041, and all of the digital clocks in the world have mysteriously stopped working.
The only solution is that an expert group of men and women, working in shifts, must click every second of the day using a mouse.
Each click advances all of the clocks in the world by one second.
The following test determines if you have the accurate clicking & timing skills needed to keep the world on time.


RULES:

  1. You must advance to the next check box in as close to one second as humanly possible.
  2. You must advance left to right, one line at a time.
  3. You must click throughout the entire hour of time with no pauses. Time stops for nobody.
  4. You and only you may do the clicking.
  5. At the end of the hour you must click the "submit" button at the bottom to recieve your score.

IMPORTANT:

  • A dot stands for a second. If you reach a check mark, that means another minute has passed.
  • Remember to breathe.1
  • Remember to hydrate prior to starting.2
  • A mouse pad is recommended.
